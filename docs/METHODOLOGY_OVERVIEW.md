# Methodology Overview - US Equity Statistical Arbitrage Engine

The engine follows a disciplined research workflow:

1. Define a statistical-dislocation hypothesis.
2. Select liquid US equities.
3. Estimate normal relationship behavior.
4. Apply signal, regime, and risk checks before entry.
5. Backtest with assumed costs.
6. Stress-test timing and execution assumptions.
7. Use paper/forward logs to review fills, spreads, slippage, and broker behavior.

US-specific work includes attention to market open/close behavior, paper-fill realism, short availability, and liquidity around intraday execution.

The public version intentionally redacts strategy-specific details.
