# Risk Disclosure

This repository is a public, redacted portfolio case study. It is not investment advice, a recommendation, or a solicitation to buy or sell any security.

The results shown are historical backtests, stress tests, paper/synthetic tests, or redacted research summaries unless explicitly stated otherwise. They do not guarantee future live performance. Real trading can differ because of slippage, spreads, rejected orders, data delays, broker/API behavior, liquidity, market gaps, outages, fees, taxes, and regime changes.

Any live deployment should start with limited allocation, independent review, clear risk limits, and execution monitoring. This repository does not create an advisory, brokerage, managed-account, or fiduciary relationship.
